About

Bringing Japan's Craft of Living to the World

For over 20 years, I've been deeply immersed in Japan's lifestyle and household goods industry — a world where everyday objects are made with extraordinary care. My mission is simple: to share Japan's culture of teineina kurashi — the art of living thoughtfully — with people around the world, through products that bring quiet joy to daily life.


Every product in this space carries a story. The story of how it was developed, the makers behind it, and the moments it creates in someone's home. I don't just connect you with suppliers — I help you understand the narrative behind each product, so you can build genuine connections with your customers.


In today's market, selling a product is not enough. What drives long-term, sustainable growth is fandom — a community of people who share your values and believe in what you offer. Together, we'll find Japanese products that resonate, and build the sourcing and import strategy that turns first-time buyers into loyal fans.
